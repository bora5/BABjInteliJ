Reports legacy BAB main menus that manually create FavTab instances, hide them, and enable them from a role switch.

The quick fix replaces the recognized createMenuItems(...) method with an immutable createMenuDefinition(). It preserves section and item order, combines roles that expose the same item, removes duplicate tab additions, and lets BAB derive visible tabs, routes, empty sections, direct-navigation checks, and safe favourites from the same definition.

The inspection only offers the fix when it can recognize the complete standard pattern. Menus with custom statements or ambiguous section placement are left unchanged.
